Airport taxi from Cranbrook, Sissinghurst, Benenden and surrounding villages

28 April 2026 · 5 min read · Areas · Coverage

The name says Hawkhurst Taxis — but we cover the entire Weald. If you're in Cranbrook, Sissinghurst, Benenden, Sandhurst, Goudhurst, or any of the surrounding villages, you can book with us at the same fixed prices and the same standard of service.

Why village pickups are our speciality

Most airport taxi services are based in town centres. Getting a reliable early-morning pickup from a rural village in Kent can be surprisingly difficult. We've always focused on the Weald specifically — we know every lane, every postcode, and every awkward turning.

Do village pickups cost more?

In most cases, no — or only marginally. We price from the village centre, so if you're on the edge of a village the extra mile or two is typically absorbed into the standard fare. For more remote addresses, we may add a small supplement — we'll always quote this up front before you confirm.
