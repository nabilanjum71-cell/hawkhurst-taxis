FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you charge a fixed price or use a meter?

Every journey is a fixed price agreed before you travel. The price you are quoted is the price you pay — we never use a meter and there are no hidden extras, no fuel surcharges, and no peak-time markups.

How far in advance do I need to book?

We can usually accommodate bookings with as little as a few hours' notice, including same-day and middle-of-the-night collections. For peak travel periods such as school holidays or Christmas, booking a week or more ahead is advisable.

Do you track flights for return pickups?

Yes. For every airport return we monitor your flight in real time. If your flight is delayed, we adjust our pickup time accordingly at no extra charge.

What vehicles do you use?

We operate a fleet of comfortable, air-conditioned saloons and estate cars, all less than five years old. Larger groups or oversized luggage can be accommodated — just mention it when booking.

Can I pay by card?

Yes — we accept cash and card payments. No surcharge applies to card payments.

Do you cover pickups from villages outside Hawkhurst?

Absolutely. We collect from Cranbrook, Sissinghurst, Goudhurst, Benenden, Sandhurst, Wadhurst, Flimwell, Ticehurst, and dozens of other villages across the Weald. See our areas page for the full list.
